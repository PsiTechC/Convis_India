How It Works

From zero to live assistant
in under 15 minutes.

01

Design your assistant

Choose a voice or chat assistant template, name it, set its personality and fallback behaviour. No coding required — configure everything from the dashboard.

02

Connect your knowledge

Upload documents, paste URLs, or connect a database or REST API. Our RAG engine chunks, embeds, and indexes your content automatically.

03

Plug in your integrations

Authorize Salesforce, Jira, HubSpot, Zoho, or any custom API in one click. Define which actions your assistant can take and what data it can access.

04

Deploy & monitor

Go live on your phone line, website widget, WhatsApp, or SMS. Every conversation is logged, transcribed, and surfaced in your analytics dashboard.
